format the sdcard in linux as gpt ext4 partion and run these comands on it's mount point
chown -R root:9997 *
chmod -R 777 *
chmod -R 2777 *

setfacl -R -m d:u::rwx,d:g::rwx,d:o::rwx *

setfacl -R -m u::rwx,g::rwx,o::rwx *




then mount the ext4 at the game mount point as android internal storage 
this is how do you get info
mount | grep emulated


the goups in android 9
root:sdcard_rw  or root:1015
the goups in android 12
u0_a202:media_rw or root:1023
